Privacy Policy for WW7ATS

Last updated: March 29, 2026

Introduction

This privacy policy describes how WW7ATS ("the App") handles your information. WW7ATS is an amateur (ham) radio Amateur Television (ATV) streaming application available on iPhone, iPad, Mac, Apple TV and Windows 11. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Camera Permission

The App requests access to your device's camera for the following purposes:

  • To capture video for live RTMP streaming to the WW7ATS ATV repeater
  • To enable real-time camera preview features
  • On Apple TV, video is captured via iPhone Continuity Camera over your local network

Important: Camera data is streamed only to the WW7ATS repeater system. No video data is sent anywhere else or stored by the developer.

Microphone Permission

The App requests access to your device's microphone for the following purposes:

  • To capture audio for live RTMP streaming to the WW7ATS ATV repeater
  • On Apple TV, audio is captured via iPhone Continuity Camera

Important: Audio data is streamed only to the WW7ATS repeater system. No audio data is sent anywhere else or stored by the developer.

Data Collection and Storage

WW7ATS does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers operated by the developer. The app does not include any analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks.

The app stores your settings (callsign, stream quality preferences) locally on your device. Your stream key is stored securely in the device Keychain (iOS/tvOS/macOS). Slideshow images are loaded temporarily into memory and are not saved or transmitted beyond the RTMP stream.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services that collect user data. The only network communication is the RTMP stream sent to the WW7ATS repeater, HLS playback of the WW7ATS feed, and (on Apple TV) the local network connection to your iPhone for Continuity Camera.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. Use of amateur radio requires an FCC license.

Your Rights

You have full control over the permissions granted to the App:

  • You can revoke camera and microphone permissions at any time through your device settings
  • The App will function within the limitations of the permissions you grant

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
