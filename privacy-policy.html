Privacy Policy for WW7ATS

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Introduction

This privacy policy describes how WW7ATS ("the App") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Camera Permission

The App requests access to your device's camera for the following purposes:

  • To capture photos and videos as part of the app's core functionality
  • To enable real-time camera preview features

Important: All camera data is processed locally on your device. We do not upload, transmit, or store any photos or videos captured through the app on external servers without your explicit consent.

Microphone Permission

The App requests access to your device's microphone for the following purposes:

  • To record audio when capturing videos
  • To enable voice-related features within the app

Important: All audio data is processed locally on your device. We do not upload, transmit, or store any audio recordings on external servers without your explicit consent.

Data Collection and Storage

WW7ATS does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. All media captured through the app remains on your device and is under your control.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services that collect user data.

Your Rights

You have full control over the permissions granted to the App:

  • You can revoke camera and microphone permissions at any time through your device settings
  • You can delete any media captured through the App at any time
  • The App will function within the limitations of the permissions you grant

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
