Privacy Policy for WW7ATS — Apple TV

Last updated: March 29, 2026

Introduction

This privacy policy describes how WW7ATS for Apple TV ("the App") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Camera Permission (Continuity Camera)

The App uses iPhone Continuity Camera to capture video for live RTMP streaming to your configured server. Your iPhone must be on the same network as your Apple TV for this feature to work.

Important: Video data captured via Continuity Camera is streamed directly to the RTMP server URL you configure in the app settings. No video data is sent anywhere else or stored on the Apple TV.

Microphone Permission

The App accesses your microphone via Continuity Camera on your iPhone for the following purpose:

  • To capture audio for live RTMP streaming to your configured server

Important: Audio data is streamed directly to the RTMP server URL you configure. No audio data is sent anywhere else or stored on the Apple TV.

Data Collection and Storage

WW7ATS does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers operated by the developer. The app does not include any analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks.

The app stores your settings (callsign, server URL, stream quality preferences) locally on your Apple TV using UserDefaults. Your RTMP stream key is stored securely in the Apple TV Keychain.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services that collect user data. The only network communication is the RTMP stream sent to the server you configure, the HLS playback from the URL you specify, and the local network connection to your iPhone for Continuity Camera.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. Use of amateur radio requires an FCC license.

Your Rights

You have full control over the permissions granted to the App:

  • You can revoke camera and microphone permissions at any time through your Apple TV settings
  • You can disconnect your iPhone from Continuity Camera at any time
  • The App will function within the limitations of the permissions you grant

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
